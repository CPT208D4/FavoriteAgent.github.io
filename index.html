Project Goal

What we’re building and why it matters

Our concept helps people manage content they collect from multiple platforms, quickly extract key points, and turn useful insights into summaries, cards, and a searchable knowledge base — making revisiting easy and reducing knowledge anxiety.

  • Centralize collections from Bilibili / Xiaohongshu / Douyin / Weibo, etc.
  • Get highlights fast (no need to read/watch everything word by word)
  • Use fragmented time with audio/short review modes
  • Tag, categorize, and retrieve saved items effortlessly

User Personas

Age · Role · Platforms · Behavior · User Expectations

Yu Chen

Bilibili · Xiaohongshu · Douyin · CSDN
Age

20

Role

University Student

Frequently Used Platforms

Bilibili, Xiaohongshu, Douyin, CSDN

Behavior

Browsing profession-related learning content every day. Their collection folder is overflowing, but the revisit rate is less than 10%. Experiences knowledge anxiety and wants to learn in a more systematic way.

User Expectations
  • Centralized management of content collected from various platforms.
  • Quickly extract key points without having to read everything word by word.
  • Utilize fragmented time during commutes or walks by listening to knowledge content.
  • Turn in-depth knowledge into memory cards and knowledge bases for easy review.
  • Be able to directly ask questions, get summaries, and generate knowledge graphs based on all the content they’ve collected.

Age

Role

Frequently Used Platforms

Behavior

Exercises intermittent effort; saves useful tips, tutorials, or interesting articles whenever they come across them. Collects dozens of items a week but almost never revisits them. Doesn’t seek depth or systematic understanding, just wants to "have seen it, know it, and be able to use it."

User Expectations

  • Quickly understand what an article or video is about.
